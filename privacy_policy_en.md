## 1. Purpose of Collection and Use, Items Collected, and Collection Method

### < Purpose of Collection and Use >

The Company processes personal information for the following purposes. The personal information being processed is not used for purposes other than the following, and if the purpose of use is changed, necessary measures such as obtaining separate consent in accordance with Article 22 of the Information and Communications Network Act will be implemented.

- **Member Management**: Confirmation of intent to sign up, user identification, confirmation of intent to withdraw, handling grievances, delivery of notices.
- **Service Provision**: Sharing chord sheets and chord information, community activities (comments, posts), providing personalized services, payment and settlement of paid services (Picks, etc.).
- **Service Improvement and Statistics**: Development of new services and provision of personalized services, analysis of service usage records and access frequency, statistics on service usage, security checks.
- **Marketing and Advertising**: Providing event information and participation opportunities, providing advertising information.
- **Disclosure of Information Among Members**: When performing community functions within the Service (comments, posts, chord sharing, etc.), **[Nickname, Profile Picture, Self-introduction, Guitar Level]** may be disclosed to other members for user identification. (However, Email, Birth Year, payment information, etc. are not disclosed.)

### < Items of Personal Information Collected >

The Company collects the minimum personal information as follows for membership registration, smooth customer consultation, and service provision.

**[Required Items]**
- **Social Login (Google, Apple, etc.)**: Social account unique identifier (ID), Email address, Name (Nickname), Profile picture URL.
- **Service Usage Process**: Service usage records, access logs, cookies, access IP information, device information (OS type, device model, app version), bad usage records.
- **Payment and Paid Services**: Payment records, usage history of virtual currency (Picks).

**[Optional Items]**
- Guitar level, self-introduction, birth year (for statistics).

*The Company does not collect personal information from children under the age of 14.*

### < Collection Method >

The Company collects personal information through the following methods:
- Sign up and social login linkage within the app.
- Automatic collection through information collection tools generated during service usage.
- Customer center consultation, event application, etc.

## 3. Retention and Use Period, Destruction Procedure and Method

### ① Retention and Use Period
In principle, user's personal information is destroyed without delay when the purpose of collection and use is achieved. However, if it is necessary to preserve it in accordance with relevant laws and regulations, user information is kept for a certain period as prescribed by relevant laws as follows.

- **Act on the Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, Etc.**
  - Records on contract or withdrawal of subscription: 5 years
  - Records on payment and supply of goods: 5 years
  - Records on consumer complaints or dispute resolution: 3 years
- **Protection of Communications Secrets Act**
  - Login records: 3 months

### ② Destruction Procedure and Method
If a user requests withdrawal of membership or withdraws consent to the collection and use of personal information, the personal information is destroyed after a grace period of 30 days (for reasons such as checking for re-registration restrictions).
- **Destruction Procedure**: Personal information for which the purpose has been achieved is stored for a certain period according to internal policies and other relevant laws and regulations and then destroyed.
- **Destruction Method**: Personal information stored in electronic file format is deleted using technical methods that cannot reproduce the record.
